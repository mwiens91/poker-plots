poker-plots generates a few interactive visualizations of our poker data. It also provides a log of all the data in nice table form.

Some features might not be obvious on first use, so click below to see what's available.

More info

Terms

  • cumulative sum: how much money a player has accumulated at a given point in time (abbreviated "cum-sum" sometimes)
  • buy-in: the total amount of money a player plays in a game
  • buy-out: the amount of money a player leaves with in a game

Features

People section
  • hovering over a player's card will highlight their data on the line plot
  • the cards also act as a legend for the player's colour
Multi-line chart
  • hovering over a player's trajectory will highlight their entire trajectory
  • hovering over a player's data point will show the data point's info on the info panel
  • hovering over either of the above will show the player's info in the info panel
  • clicking on a player's data point will scroll down to the corresponding game
  • scrolling will zoom in the x-axis
  • dragging will pan on the x-axis
Box plot
  • hovering over a player's "box and whiskers" will show the extrema and quartiles
  • hovering over an outlier will show its value
Pie charts
  • hovering over a player's arc or label will show their current cumulative sum and percentage of total winnings/losses

About

This page was written by Matt Wiens. D3.js is used for data visualization and the most of the styling is courtesy of Bootstrap. Ancient browsers and Safari might not be supported. Mobile is going to be a terrible experience. The source code is available here.
